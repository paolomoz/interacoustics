Our Commitment to Sustainability

Learn about the active steps we are taking to reduce our environmental footprint and carbon emissions, including product design, disposal, packaging and more.

Improving energy efficiency in production and logistics

Engaging suppliers to reduce upstream emissions

Supporting net-zero ambitions across the value chain

We continuously optimize packaging to reduce waste and increase recycled content:

Cardboard packaging contains at least 98% recycled material

Plastic bags contain a minimum of 50% recycled content

Bubble foil is made from 100% ocean waste plastic

Stretch foil for pallets contains 80% recycled material

We work closely with suppliers to improve sustainability across the value chain. This includes:

Collecting data on subcomponents

Reducing the use of virgin materials

Exploring more sustainable logistics

Involving suppliers in our Life-cycle analysis
