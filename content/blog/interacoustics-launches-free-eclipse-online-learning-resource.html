Interacoustics is excited to introduce a new, free online onboarding resource designed to help you get the most from your Eclipse evoked potentials system.

Interacoustics is excited to introduce a new, free online onboarding resource designed to help you get the most from your Eclipse evoked potentials system. The platform brings together essential product training in one accessible hub, supporting both new and experienced users.

The resource features structured learning modules and visual demonstrations that simplify complex procedures and enable you to learn at your own pace. From unboxing and installation to software configuration and protocol setup, the tool provides clear, step-by-step guidance to ensure efficient and accurate system use.

The resource is suitable for onboarding new staff, refreshing existing knowledge, and standardizing clinical workflows. It also includes guidance on performing a wide range of tests, from threshold ABR to vestibular evoked myogenic potentials (VEMPs).

By improving confidence and reducing setup time, the resource aims to help you focus on delivering high-quality patient care.

To get started, you must enter the first seven digits of your Eclipse serial number. Access the free online onboarding resource here or scan the QR code below to start learning today.
