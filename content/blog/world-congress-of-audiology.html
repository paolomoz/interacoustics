We're excited to meet with colleagues and partners in person at the 37th World Congress of Audiology, scheduled for May 24-27 in Seoul, South Korea.

We look forward to discussing the latest advancements within diagnostic audiology and balance with you, where you’ll have ample opportunity to demo our cutting-edge solutions and chat with our expert staff. Make sure to meet us at booth C-1 (hall B2)!

Stop by our booth C-1 (hall B2) for the unique opportunity to take part in hands-on, in-booth workshops using the latest technology:

May 25, 14.00-14.30: Equinox Evo and Touch Keyboard, including the Audible Contrast Threshold (ACT™) test.

May 26, 10.00-10.30: Functional Vision Head Impulse Test (fvHIT™) using the VORTEQ™ head sensor.

Below, you can find a few of the presentations and workshops we recommend by our in-house clinicians and research partners.

Time: May 24, 13.00-14.20.

Moderators: Andy J. Beynon, Ph.D. and Karen C. Hendrick, Au.D.

Time: May 24, 16.20-17.40.

Moderator: Devin L. McCaslin, Ph.D.

Time: May 25, 12.00-12.15.

Presenter: Devin L. McCaslin, Ph.D.

Related article: A new medical device could help diagnose infants with vestibular issues.

Time: May 25, 15.08-15.16 (one presentation among several on auditory processing during 14.50-16.10).

Presenter: Rezarta Bardhi, MSc Audiology.

Related research: Evaluating the user-operated audible contrast threshold test as a language-independent proxy of speech-in-noise perception.

Time: May 26, 11.50-12.05 (one session among several on speech audiometry during 11.20-12.40).

Presenter: Johannes Zaar, Ph.D.
