Going to APTA CSM 2026 in Anaheim, CA? Then make sure to visit our booth and discover the latest solutions for comprehensive diagnostics and rehabilitation.

We're excited to meet with colleagues and partners in person at APTA CSM 2026, scheduled for February 12-14 in Anaheim, CA. Meet us at booth 207 at this year's conference.

Envision your clinic’s experience when you use Interacoustics equipment to comprehensively care for your patients from start to finish. Stop by our booth to move through the DATA (Diagnose, Assess, Train, Assess) patient journey using exciting technologies such as VisualEyes™, VORTEQ™, the Virtualis virtual reality rehabilitation solutions, and much more!

Virtualis is an exciting member of the Interacoustics portfolio and offers clinicians comprehensive tools for the assessment and rehabilitation of patients with vestibular/balance, neurologic, and musculoskeletal disorders. Stop by our booth, where you will have the unique opportunity to demo the entire Virtualis line and its many modules covering a wide array of rehabilitation needs.

In addition to the MotionVR system, which combines virtual reality with a dynamic 360-degree force plate to assess the entire balance system and train adaptation, substitution, and habituation strategies, you can also demo PhysioVR and StaticVR to see which setup would be right for your clinic.

Our in-house physical therapists, vestibular clinicians, and balance specialists look forward to seeing you for a chat about the latest advancements and how our solutions could fit into your practice.

Make sure to stop by our booth and enter our prize draws:

Daily giveaway drawing for Apple AirPods.

Daily Starbucks gift card (see more details below).

Grand prize drawing for HTC VIVE Focus Vision VR headset on February 14.

Stop by booth 207 for a game of ‘Whac-A-Mush’ – a rehabilitation module available with the PhysioVR software – and see how many virtual mushrooms you can successfully whack. Each day's highest scorer will receive a Starbucks gift card.

During July 14-16, 2026, we are delighted to invite you to a 3-day, in-person workshop on vestibular diagnostics, hosted at the Interacoustics US office in Eden Prairie, MN. Attendees will learn how to use a variety of diagnostic tools to evaluate and treat the balance system. Content covers diagnostic vestibular tests, functional assessments, and interpretation of different vestibular test results. Instruction includes clinical instruction, interactive discussions, how-to demonstrations, and a series of practical rotations for attendees to gain hands-on experience and skills.
