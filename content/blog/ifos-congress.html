It was a pleasure for Interacoustics to participate at IFOS 2023 in Dubai.

Following the last IFOS congress in Paris 2017, the long-awaited IFOS congress in Dubai 2023 is just around the corner, January 17-21 at Dubai World Trade Centre.

The theme for IFOS 2023 is “Connecting minds, creating a future for a better tomorrow” and will cover a comprehensive scientific program within:

And many more exciting disciplines.

We are delighted to have three of our in-house audiologists speaking at this year’s congress: Jack Bennett and Leigh Martin from the Interacoustics Academy, and Michelle Petrak, Ph.D.

All times stated are in GMT+4.

In a BPPV section on January 18, 10.30-11.30, Leigh Martin will discuss the role of repositioning chairs in benign paroxysmal positional vertigo (BPPV).

Related Interacoustics Academy course: The role of repositioning chairs in the diagnosis and treatment of BPPV

In a roundtable on January 19, 17.30-18.30, Dr. Michelle Petrak will give her talk on “Clinical Saccadometry: Establishing Normative Thresholds using a Simplified Videonystagmography Protocol in the Adult Population.”

Related Interacoustics Academy course: Saccadometry – a new tool for assessing central vestibular disorders

On January 20, 12.00-13.00, Jack Bennett will host an instructional course on correction factors in threshold ABR and ASSR.

Related Interacoustics Academy course: ABR Correction Factors

Interacoustics will also be exhibiting at IFOS 2023 – stop by booth 56 to learn how our equipment can benefit you in your clinical practice.

We will gladly provide demonstrations of:

The TRV repositioning chair

The VisualEyes™ VNG system

And more. We look forward to speaking with you.
