New single point of entry to Interacoustics software suites for a smooth, fast and efficient workflow.

Interacoustics Universe™ is a new single point of entry to several of Interacoustics’ software suites. It runs through Noah and lets the hearing care professional easily shift between the various suites without having to close them.

As an example, you can launch the Viot™ Suite to perform video otoscopy, then quickly move to the Titan Suite to perform tympanometry, and then quickly move back to the Affinity Suite for your audiometry.

The single point of entry and multiple measurement opportunities provide a more flexible and seamless workflow.

Interacoustics Universe™ also allows for switching clients without having to close any open suites. The data flow is managed automatically to ensure that data is allocated to the correct client in Noah.

The new single point of entry makes the hearing care professional’s workflow in the software much faster and smoother than today. The hearing care professional will save time and it reduces the waiting time of clients who will also experience the entire process as much smoother and more professional.

Interacoustics has released Interacoustics Universe™ to several of their suites, such as Affinity, Callisto™, Viot™, Titan, IA OAE, and Diagnostic suites. More will be added later.
