Meet our new Interacoustics employees from April 2022. We're always on the lookout for fresh talent, so don't hold back reaching out to us.

Anne Louise and her team will lead all the great development projects and continue to improve the field of project management at Interacoustics. Anne Louise likes to spend time in the nature – either running or walking with her dog.
