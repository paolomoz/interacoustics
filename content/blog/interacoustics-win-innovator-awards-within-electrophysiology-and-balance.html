Interacoustics has been recognized in the 2025 Hearing Health & Technology Matters Innovator Awards, winning in both electrophysiology and balance. Learn...

We’re proud to share that Interacoustics has been recognized in the 2025 Hearing Health & Technology Matters Innovator Awards, winning in both:

Electrophysiology for the Eclipse Aided Cortical module

Balance for the VORTEQ™ Functional Assessments bundle

The Eclipse Aided Cortical module, powered by the ManU-IRU stimuli and Fmpi™ detector developed by the Interacoustics Research Unit (IRU), provides an objective way to validate hearing aid benefit in patients that cannot complete behavioral testing.

The VORTEQ™ Functional Assessments bundle enables clinicians to assess the vestibulo-ocular reflex (VOR) with precision, helping physical therapists tailor rehabilitation programs. It also includes the newly patented Functional Vision Head Impulse Test (fvHIT™).

These awards are a testament to our dedication to delivering life-changing hearing and balance health through research-based innovation and reflect our commitment to ‘Science made smarter’.
