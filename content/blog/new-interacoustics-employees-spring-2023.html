Meet our new Interacoustics employees during Spring 2023. We're always on the lookout for fresh talent, so don't hold back reaching out to us.

We are excited to share the faces of our 9 new colleagues who have recently joined the great Interacoustics team.

Emilie is a recent graduate of English Studies from the Southern University of Denmark and will be our new Personal Assistant. Her job is to provide executive support, including planning and travel bookings, and she’ll play a big role in organizing the work around our new office building that will soon be constructed next to our current building. In her spare time, Emilie enjoys rhythm gymnastics and is a children’s gymnastics coach. She also loves to spend time with family and friends.

Joseph joined us in May as Software Developer of shared product components. In his spare time, he enjoys reading about science and technology, listening to music, and watching movies.

Kim has joined our Hardware-Mechanical team as Mechanical Engineer. He’ll be part of our probe program working with optimizations and new probes. In his spare time, he enjoys cycling, books, and spending time in his garden.

Lasse has joined us as Director of Hearing and Balance Consumables to drive our consumables and accessories business.

Being a father of three children, Lasse has his hands full in his spare time with coaching and supporting motocross driving. Also, he spends a great deal of time refurbishing his old house and likes to go running and mountain biking.

Mathias is one of many new engineers who has joined us to develop and maintain core software modules. He loves skiing and mountain biking, but since he’s also a recent father, he spends most of his time with his baby, girlfriend and not least the dog.

Nick is one of two new Junior Software Developers. He’ll be implementing new features and functionality in our product software. This includes writing new code, troubleshooting and fixing existing code. Nick enjoys gaming and spending time with family or friends.

Niels recently joined us as Embedded Software Developer as part of our Embedded Software Team. Niels will be working on the development of new modules and firmware solutions that can be used in future and existing products. In his spare time, he hangs out with friends, plays badminton and paddle, reads, and plays Counter Strike.

Per has joined our Firmware Team as Senior Embedded Software Developer being responsible for firmware development in various projects. In his spare time, he likes running, motor cycling and gardening.

Sara recently joined us as Junior Software Developer where she will be part of our balance software team, continuing the development of our popular VisualEyes™ VNG system. Sara is an avid gamer, both digital and analog, and she also likes to go swimming. In her own words, she’s a keen abuser of the podcast section of Spotify and she likes to do “mystery” puzzles.
