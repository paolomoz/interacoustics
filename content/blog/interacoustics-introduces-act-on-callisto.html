The game-changing and award-winning Audible Contrast Threshold (ACT™) test is now available with the Callisto™ in addition to the Affinity Compact. Learn...

Interacoustics is excited to announce that the game-changing Audible Contrast Threshold (ACT) test is now available with the Callisto. This means that Callisto users can address the number one complaint from people with hearing loss: hearing in noise.

ACT is a language-independent test that measures a client’s hearing-in-noise ability in less than 2 minutes. It’s a personalized test that applies the client’s audiogram to ensure audibility of the ACT stimuli. In this way, hearing care professionals can now perform a robust assessment that reflects a person’s real-world hearing abilities.

The outcome of the ACT test – the ACT value – can quickly and efficiently support the fitting of advanced hearing aid features, allowing the client to hear better in noisy environments.

With ACT on both Affinity Compact and Callisto, Interacoustics provides the possibility to offer a new standard of care for more hearing aid clinics.
