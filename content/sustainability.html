Founded on care

Interacoustics began by supporting schools for the deaf and continues to improve lives through advanced hearing and balance equipment for healthcare professionals worldwide.

Committed to hearing healthcare

We support global health and well-being, aligned with the UN’s sustainability goals. Our tools help detect hearing loss and treat balance disorders, enabling better quality of life.

Driven by research

Through global collaborations and our own Research Unit at DTU, we develop innovative methods to meet real-world challenges in hearing care.

Sustainability activities

Today, our caring perspective goes beyond the hearing healthcare industry. We feel obliged to care for our surroundings, the environment and the local communities in which we are based.

Our sustainable activities are centered around:

  • Environment & climate
  • Society & local community
  • People & Culture
  • Business ethics & governance

Environment & Climate

We have a clear ambition to reduce our environmental footprint. We are constantly finding ways to reduce our CO2 emissions and to better utilize waste and hard-to-decompose substances.

Reducing our footprint

Goals and initiatives include reducing packaging material, using recyclable materials in both products and packaging, and initiating product life cycle assessments (LCAs).

Society & Local Community

Interacoustics supports hearing healthcare communities through donations and partnerships that improve access to care and education.

We’ve donated equipment to the Norwegian Hard of Hearing’s program in Malawi, enabling hearing tests in rural areas and training at a school for hearing-impaired children.

Business Ethics & Governance

Supply management

At Interacoustics we comply with all rules and regulations, and we always strive to conduct our business with high ethical standards. For instance, we ensure that our suppliers live up to our high morals on business ethics, anti-corruption and labor practice. Our suppliers are selected carefully and in accordance with our Supplier Code of Conduct.
