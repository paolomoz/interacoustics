Turn on Titan by pressing the R or L button.

Open the OtoAccess or Noah database and enter new patient details.

Double click on the Titan Suite icon to launch the software and click on the ABRIS module tab.

Select the desired test protocol from the dropdown list.

Select the ear for testing.

The ideal test environment is a quiet room where lights and other electronic equipment are turned off.

The infant should be sleeping or in quiet relaxed state.

Sucking, blinking, crying or movement may affect testing.
