Standard parts

Power supply: UES60LCP-200300SPC

Power splitter cable, 2 meters

USB-C to USB-A adapter (for use with USB-A ports)

Ensure the Touch Keyboard is charged before use. Only use the supplied charger or a PC’s USB port to charge the Touch Keyboard.

Note: The direct USB-C connection may not provide sufficient power or may result in slower charging. USB-A ports will not provide sufficient power to charge the keyboard.

1. Connect the USB-C cable to the Touch Keyboard.

2. Connect to a USB-C port on the PC.

3. If needed, use the USB-C to USB-A adapter.
