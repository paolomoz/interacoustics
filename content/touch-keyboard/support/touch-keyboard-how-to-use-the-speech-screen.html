Touch Keyboard Support

This is how to use the speech screen on the Touch Keyboard.

You can start and stop your speech control by using two dedicated buttons in the lower right-hand corner to score your speech control. You can use the dedicated scoring buttons on the touch screen.

In the top left corner of the screen, you have information about channel one. You can see the intensity, ear side, transducer, and stimuli. To change these, click the drop-down button. This presents you with different options.

On the right side of the screen, you have information about channel two. To enable this, you increase the intensity. To turn this off, you decrease the intensity. The mask icon tells you if channel two is currently being used as a masking channel. Click the drop-down menu to view and select other options for channel two.

In the middle of the screen, you can see the two previous words, the current word that is being played, and the two next words in the list. Under each word that has been played, you can see the score.

In the top middle of the screen, you have information about the speech score and word count. You also have information about the speech material and word list. To store the results, you can either use the record button or the button on the rotary dial.

In the central dropdown, you can select your speech material and your word list.
