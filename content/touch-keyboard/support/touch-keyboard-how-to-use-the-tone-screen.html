Touch Keyboard Support

This is how to use the tone screen on the Touch Keyboard.

In the home screen, you have two dedicated areas that work as tone switches.

You have your frequency selector in the middle.

To start a point, click the button by the tone switch on the left rotary dial.

To enable ‘Talk Forward’, click the button on the top of the left rotary dial.

To disable ‘Talk Forward’, you click the same button again.

On the upper left side of the screen, you have information dedicated to Channel 1.

You can see when a stimulus is being presented.
