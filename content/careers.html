What is it like working for Interacoustics?

At Interacoustics, we combine modern workspaces, a strong social culture, and world‑leading innovation to create an inspiring workplace for our employees.

Fun and social activities

We prioritize well‑being and strong social connections. From fitness runs and bike rides to Friday breakfasts, office events, and massages, there’s always something to join and someone to connect with.

Leading in innovation

We turn ideas into real solutions. At Interacoustics, you’ll collaborate with skilled colleagues and use modern tools to create technology that supports hearing and balance professionals worldwide.

Bright and open office spaces

Our award‑winning building offers an open, light‑filled workspace designed for both focus and collaboration. It’s a flexible environment where ideas can grow and flow naturally.

Powered by the Demant Group
