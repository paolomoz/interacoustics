Help your clients hear better in noise

Do your clients often complain they cannot hear and understand what people are saying in noisy environments – even with their hearing aids on? ACT is a revolutionary test that quickly provides you with insights into your client’s hearing ability in noise. By performing ACT in your clients, you will be in a much better position to help them perform well in noisy environments.

Using the same setup as for audiometry, you can perform the ACT test right after the audiogram.

ACT is an evidence-based test invented by the Interacoustics Research Unit (IRU). It is based on years of research with leading scientists and universities around the world.
