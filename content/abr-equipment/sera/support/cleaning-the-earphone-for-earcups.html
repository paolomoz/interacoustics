The insert phones (transducer boxes, cables, tubes and adapters) can be wiped down with a damp cloth and a disinfectant wipe after use.

Be careful that no fluid enters into the adapters or sensitive areas.

Step 1: Wipe down the adapters.

Step 2: Wipe down the tubes.Examine the tubes periodically for any cracks in the tubing. Replace a cracked tube with a new one.

Step 3: Wipe down the transducer boxes and cable.

Before reuse: The insert earphone components should be completely dry before reuse.

Accessories: If necessary, replace the tubes and adapters.Tube length is critical to stimulus calibration. Do not modify the length of the tubing in any way.
