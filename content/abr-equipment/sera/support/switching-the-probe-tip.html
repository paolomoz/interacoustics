Switching the Probe Tip

Learn how to switch the Sera™ probe tip.

Sera™ Support

Step 1: Unscrew the probe cap.

Step 2: Remove the probe tip. Make sure there is a gasket in the clean probe tip.

Step 3: Replace with a clean/replacement tip.

Step 4: Assemble the probe again (step 2+1).

Notice: Clean the probe tip regularly

To avoid earwax clogging the tubes of the probe tip and affecting measurements, it is recommended that you clean the probe tip regularly.

Complementary probe tip enclosed

Please find a complementary probe tip enclosed with your system purchase.
