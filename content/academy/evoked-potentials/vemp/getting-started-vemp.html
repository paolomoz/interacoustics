In this course, you'll be introduced to VEMP testing, including both cVEMP and oVEMP. The course covers patient preparation, instructions, and more. Learn...

This course focuses on how to get started for both cVEMP and oVEMP testing. Topics covered include cover patient preparation, patient instructions, protocol and parameter selection and possible modifications to testing.

Learning Objectives

  • Explain the differences between cVEMP and oVEMP pathways
  • Describe how to prepare patients for cVEMP and oVEMP testing
  • Describe the modifications possible for cVEMP and oVEMP testing
