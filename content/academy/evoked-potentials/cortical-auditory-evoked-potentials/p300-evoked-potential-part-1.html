In this two-part series on the P300 evoked potential, we explore its basic principles, how to measure it, and its applications.

This video will explore the basic principles of the P300 event-related potential. The relationship between P300 and N1-P2 and MMN waveforms will be discussed. This video will explain key consideratons for stimulation and recording of the P300, as well as discussing the potential clinical applications of this measurement.
