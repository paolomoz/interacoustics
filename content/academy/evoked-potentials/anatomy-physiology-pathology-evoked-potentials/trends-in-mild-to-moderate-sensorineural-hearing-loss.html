Learn about the etiology and prevalence of mild to moderate hearing losses, and the latest clinical approaches and advances in their diagnosis and management.

This course will explore the latest perspectives on the assessment and management of mild to moderate sensorineural hearing losses in pediatric patients. This course is taken from the 3rd Trends in Pediatrics 3-day course hosted in May 2022.

Learning Objectives

  • Explain the aetiology and prevalence of mild to moderate hearing losses
  • Describe the latest clinical approaches to diagnosing mild to moderate hearing losses
  • Compare recent advances in managing mild to moderate hearing losses
