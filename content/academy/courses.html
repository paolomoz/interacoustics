Take on-demand, online audiology courses within ABR, audiometry, balance, hearing aid fitting, and tympanometry. Free of charge. Certificates upon completion.

These courses are open to anyone who wishes to take them and are free of charge to access. Upon completion you will receive a certificate for your continuing professional development portfolio. Courses labeled CEU AAA are approved by American Academy of Audiology for CEU points. If you are a member of AAA then CEU points will be submitted automatically on your behalf within 30 days.
