This webinar covers the two different methods of measuring a real ear to coupler difference (RECD), including a live demonstration.

In this webinar, Leigh Martin will introduce Real Ear to Coupler Difference measurements and describe two different methods of how to perform them. Leigh also has a live demonstration of the SPL method and runs through RECD interpretation and troubleshooting
