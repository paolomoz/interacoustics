In this course, you'll learn about CROS/BiCROS fittings and how to perform verification of CROS and BiCROS hearing instruments.

This course will demonstrate how to perform verification of CROS and BiCROS hearing instruments. The session will focus on which patient candidacy for CROS/BiCROS fittings, the benefits and advantages of CROS/BiCROS fittings over traditional devices, and how to utilise probe microphone measurements in order to fit and verify such fittings.

Learning Objectives

  • Describe the patient groups that are suitable for CROS/BiCROS fittings
  • Describe the benefits of CROS/BiCROS hearing aid fittings
  • Explain how to perform probe microphone measurements for CROS/BiCROS fittings
