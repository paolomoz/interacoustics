In this webinar, Navid Shahnaz, Ph.D., will introduce you to wideband acoustic immittance (WAI), also known as wideband tympanometry (WBT).

In this video Dr Navid Shahnaz explains Wideband Acoustic Immittance (WAI) and its similarities and differences to conventional tympanometry. He then goes onto explain how to analzye WBT patterns in normal and different middle ear pathologies. Lastly he identifies the limitation of middle ear analysis techniques and identify areas for future research direction.
