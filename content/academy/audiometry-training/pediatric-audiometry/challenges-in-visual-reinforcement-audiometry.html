This video discusses some of the biggest challenges faced when performing Visual Reinforcement Audiometry (VRA).

In this video, Michael East and Amanda Goodhew discuss their experiences of performing Visual Reinforcement Audiometry (VRA) and reflect on some of the biggest challenges they faced in doing so.

To learn more, please visit the following interactive course, where Michael and Amanda will explore VRA and the challenges facing clinicians performing this test. Best practices and guidelines will be discussed to support performing effective and high-quality testing. Technological advances designed to simplify the clinician’s experience and optimize the test technique will be presented through video demonstration and case studies.
