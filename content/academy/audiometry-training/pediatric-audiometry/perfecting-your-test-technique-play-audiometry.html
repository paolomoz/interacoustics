This course will explore play audiometry and the challenges facing clinicians performing this test. On-demand and free of charge.

This session will explore play audiometry and the challenges facing clinicians performing this test. Suggestions, ideas, tips and tricks will be proposed to help clinicians elevate their testing technique. Technological advances designed to simplify the clinician’s experience, minimize cueing and focus more closely on the patient will be presented through video demonstration and case studies.

Learning Objectives

  • Identify the challenges facing clinicians when performing play audiometry
  • Describe the considerations and strategies available for performing high quality play audiometry
  • Describe how to implement the technological advances in play audiometry in order to facilitate the acquisition of accurate results
