This course will explore visual reinforcement audiometry (VRA) and the challenges facing clinicians performing this test. On-demand and free of charge.

This course will explore visual reinforcement audiometry and the challenges facing clinicians performing this test. Best practice and guidelines will be discussed to support performing effective and high-quality testing. Technological advances designed to simplify the clinician’s experience and optimize the test technique will be presented through video demonstration and case studies.

Describe how to implement the technological advances in visual reinforcement audiometry in order to facilitate the acquisition of accurate results

Learning Objectives

  • Identify the challenges facing clinicians when performing visual reinforcement audiometry
  • Describe the best practices and guidance for performing high quality visual reinforcement audiometry
