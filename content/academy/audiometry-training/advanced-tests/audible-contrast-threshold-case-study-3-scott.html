In this video, Leigh Martin, Director of Interacoustics Academy, and Michael East, International Clinical Trainer, explore clinical case studies that demonstrate the use of the Audible Contrast Threshold (ACT) test in more advanced scenarios.They discuss important testing considerations for complex presentations, answer frequently asked questions, and explore the clinical management implications of a range of ACT results.This video is part of the Interacoustics Academy course "Beyond The Basics: Audible Contrast Threshold".
