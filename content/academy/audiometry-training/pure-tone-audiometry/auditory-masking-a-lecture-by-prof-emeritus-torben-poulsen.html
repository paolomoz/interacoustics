During this talk, Prof. Poulsen presents a model of frequency selectivity, discusses the bandwidth of auditory filters, and more.

The Interacoustics Academy welcomed Torben Poulsen, Professor Emeritus, from the Department of Electrical Engineering at the Technical University of Denmark to discuss auditory masking.

In this presentation, you will learn about the upward spread of masking, the motion of the basilar membrane within the cochlea, the differences between complete and partial masking, and temporal aspects such as forward and backward masking.
