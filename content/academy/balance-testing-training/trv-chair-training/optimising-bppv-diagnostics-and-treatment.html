In this course, you will learn how to optimize BPPV diagnostics and review the evidence base for using the TRV Chair in BPPV patients.

Welcome to this course on diagnosing and treating BPPV with a focus on repositioning chairs. It formed part of the Trends in Balance 2022 event hosted by the Interacoustics Academy in September 2022. By completing this course you will achieve the following learning outcomes.

Learning Objectives

  • Understanding how to optimise BPPV diagnostics protocols using VNG goggles and the TRV chair
  • Understand how augmented treatment protocols can improve BPPV treatments
  • Review the evidence base for using the TRV chair in treating BPPV
