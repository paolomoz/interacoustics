In this video, you will learn how to perform BPPV diagnostics using the TRV Chair.

In this video Dr Dan Dupont Hougaard describes his diagnostic procedure for identifying BPPV using the TRV chair. In the video he covers his multi step approach including a gross eye movement exam, VOR suppression, Dix Hallpike procedure and head roll test. This video recorded at Aalborg University Hospital using the VisualEyes 525 VNG system and the TRV chair.
