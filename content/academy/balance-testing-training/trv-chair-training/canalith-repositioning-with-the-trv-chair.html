Learn about the pathophysiology, diagnostic techniques, and treatment options for BPPV and how the TRV Chair can improve both diagnosis and treatment of BPPV.

Dr. Thomas Richard-Vitton, the inventor of the TRV Chair, discusses the pathophysiology and diagnostic techniques for benign paroxysmal positional vertigo (BPPV). Dr. Richard-Vitton also demonstrates the treatment options for BPPV and how the TRV Chair can improve both diagnosis and treatment of this condition.
