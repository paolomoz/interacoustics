Learn about the pathways involved in the VNG test battery, the test procedure without fixation, spontaneous nystagmus, and positional nystagmus.

This videos intended learning outcomes are to determine the optimal way to perform gaze, spontaneous nystagmus, and static position tests, to identify different types of nystagmus and their clinical significance and differentiate between central and non-central types of nystagmus.
