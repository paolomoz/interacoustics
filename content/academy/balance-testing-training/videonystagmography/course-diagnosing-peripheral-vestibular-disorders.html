Explore causes of peripheral vestibular dysfunction, how to diagnose these conditions, and how they present themselves in different vestibular tests.

Leigh Martin, Interacoustics Academy Global Manager, will walk you through the most common causes of peripheral vertigo and look at new and emerging techniques which can be used to diagnose these conditions.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the most common causes of peripheral vestibular dysfunction
  • Understand the diagnostic tests that can be used to diagnose these conditions
  • Understand how these conditions present themselves in the different diagnostic tests available
