In this course, you'll learn how to minimize artifacts in your VNG testing to ensure accurate results, including how to calibrate your VNG system. Learn more.

In this course, Dr. Kamran Barin will explore different types of artifacts that are found within VNG testing. He will start with an explanation of how to correctly calibrate a VNG system and what to look for to establish calibration has been performed successful. He will then look at the different subtests of the VNG test battery and highlight common artifacts and their causes. Lastly, he will instruct on VNG best practices and how to minimize artifacts while testing.

Identify different types of artifacts in VNG.

Identify calibration errors in VNG.

Describe best practices to minimize the effect of artifacts.
