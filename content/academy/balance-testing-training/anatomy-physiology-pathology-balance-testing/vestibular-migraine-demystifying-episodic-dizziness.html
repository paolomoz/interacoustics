Learn how to identify reported symptoms and features of vestibular migraine (VM), mechanisms related to VM, and treatment approaches to help VM patients.

Learners will be able to acquire knowledge to identify reported symptoms and features of Vestibular migraine (VM) when talking a patient’s clinical history. Dr Kaski will present and critically analyse both published data from the field of neurology and neurophysiology to increase understand of the mechanisms related to vestibular migraine and share his own clinical experience on treatment approaches to help patients manage their symptoms of VM.

Evaluate the present understanding of VM pathophysiology

Learning Objectives

  • Explain the clinical features of vestibular migraine (VM)
  • Discuss current treatment approaches of VM
