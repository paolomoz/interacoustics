The vestibular assessment toolbox offers a wide range of tests that can be used to clinically evaluate the dizzy patient. But which tools should you use and when? At the end of this session learners will understand what is considered a comprehensive vestibular assessment, and the importance of diagnostic information to both determine the diagnosis and assess the impact of the pathology in patients with dizziness and or balance impairment.

Evaluation of the assessments for comprehensive vestibular assessment

Analyse the relationship between vestibular impairment and functional changes in balance

Critique case studies to describe the application of a comprehensive clinical assessment
