Learn how to perform functional balance assessments in your clinic and use this information to bridge the gap between vestibular diagnostics and...

Objective vestibular assessment provides a valuable insight into the physiological function of the vestibular system. However, vestibular abnormalities will often have vastly different functional impacts upon individuals, highlighting the need for more function-focused vestibular test approach. Join us in this eWorkshop to explore how you can implement and optimize functional balance assessments in your clinics and use this information to bridge the gap between vestibular diagnostics and rehabilitation.

Describe methods to assess the Vestibular Ocular Reflex, Gaze Stability and functional balance/postural control.

Explain how functional vestibular assessment contributes to the successful management of patient's with vestibular disorders.

Analyse vestibular function test results and review the way in which functional balance assessment and virtual reality contributes to comprehensive balance assessment.
