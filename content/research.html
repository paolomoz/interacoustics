Interacoustics is committed to advancing hearing and balance healthcare through innovative technology and research.

From research to innovative diagnostic solutions

By researching and exploring new technologies and methods in the field of audiology, we can bring cutting-edge solutions to the market.

1 - Translational research

Bridging the gap between early science discoveries and practical application in a product

2 - Further maturation of research concept

Refining and developing a new test method or test

3 - Clinical validation

Verifying the clinical safety and effectiveness of a new test

4 - New solutions

Implementation and release of a new test in an existing or new product

The end goals and outputs are improved test accuracy and efficiency, enhanced patient experiences, and better hearing and balance outcomes for patients worldwide.
