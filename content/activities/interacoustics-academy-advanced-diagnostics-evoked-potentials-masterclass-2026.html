Interacoustics Academy Advanced Diagnostics Evoked Potentials Masterclass 2026

Sign up

Join us for a 3-day in-person masterclass at Interacoustics headquarters, designed to advance your expertise in diagnostic evoked potentials testing.

Location: Middelfart, Denmark

Join us for a 3-day in-person masterclass at Interacoustics headquarters, designed to advance your expertise in diagnostic evoked potentials testing. This immersive program combines theoretical insights with hands-on training, ensuring you leave with practical skills and confidence. Essential pre-learning materials will be shared with participants to be studied in advance of the course.

Threshold ABR, ASSR and neurological ABR / neuro-rate study examinationsCortical testing for threshold assessment, discrimination ability (MMN / P300), objective hearing aid validation and ANSD assessment and managementElectrocochleography and VEMP testing, eABR and research applications of evoked potentialsCase studies, live demonstrations, and practical sessions using state-of-the-art equipment

Audiologists, otolaryngologists, audiovestibular physicians, and healthcare professionals seeking to refresh or enhance their diagnostic evoked potential assessment skills.Prerequisite: A general understanding of evoked potentials testing and prior clinical experience.

Price: €1000 + €250 VAT (VAT refund instructions: https://skat.dk)

✔ 3-day workshop including interactive course notes ✔ Hotel accommodation (10 – 12 November)✔ Breakfast, lunch, and coffee breaks✔ 1 course dinner✔ Transportation between hotel and office

Diploma Access for discounted price

We are pleased to offer course participants a special discounted price for the Interacoustics Academy Clinical Diploma: Newborn Hearing Assessment. Usually priced at 800 euros, participants will be able to register for the Clinical Diploma for just 200 euros. This offer must be paid for upon registering for the course. For more information about the Clinical Diploma, please visit https://www.interacoustics.com/academy/clinical-diploma
