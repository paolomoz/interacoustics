Join us for a 3-day in-person masterclass at Interacoustics headquarters, designed to advance your expertise in pediatric audiology beyond the newborn stage.

Location: Middelfart, Denmark

Join us for a 3-day in-person masterclass at Interacoustics headquarters, designed to advance your expertise in pediatric audiology beyond the newborn stage. This immersive program combines theoretical insights with hands-on training, ensuring you leave with practical skills and confidence.

Behavioural assessment techniques: Visual Reinforcement Audiometry (VRA), play audiometry, and speech testingMiddle ear function testing, otoacoustic emissions, ABR and ASSR threshold estimationAided cortical testing and hearing aid fitting & managementLatest innovations and best practices in pediatric audiology for 2026Case studies, live demonstrations, and practical sessions using state-of-the-art equipment

Audiologists, otolaryngologists, audiovestibular physicians, and healthcare professionals seeking to refresh or enhance their pediatric audiology skills.Prerequisite: A general understanding of pediatric audiology and prior clinical experience.

Price: €1000 + €250 VAT (VAT refund instructions: https://skat.dk)

✔ 3-day workshop including interactive course notes ✔ Hotel accommodation 3 nights (14th 15th and 16th September)✔ Breakfast, lunch, and coffee breaks✔ 1 course dinner✔ Transportation between hotel and office

Usually priced at 800 euros, participants will be able to register for the Clinical Diploma for just 200 euros. This offer must be paid for upon registering for the course.
