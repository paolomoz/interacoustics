How to perform CROS

This quick guide is intended to give a brief explanation on how to fit CROS and BiCROS type hearing aids.

1. Launch Affinity/Callisto Suite.

2. Ensure audiogram is entered.

3. Navigate to REM module.

4. Fitting prescription settings will automatically pop up, click ‘Okay’.

5. Ensure Monaural view is selected.

6. Ensure Response view is selected (Figure 1).
