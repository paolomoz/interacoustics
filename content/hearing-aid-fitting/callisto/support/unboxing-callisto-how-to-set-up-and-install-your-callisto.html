This video explains what you receive with your Callisto™ and how you set up and install the device.

Welcome to your new Interacoustics Callisto. In this video, we will guide you through what's included in the box and how to connect your device so you're ready to get started quickly and confidently.

Start by opening the Callisto carrying case. Inside, you will find all the components you need and your device’s calibration folder. You can store this calibration folder inside the carry case.

Next, remove all the components from the case and keep hold of any packaging as this may be needed when repacking the device for annual calibration. Inside the box, you will find the following hardware:

DD65 circumaural headphones

The loudspeaker splitter cable

The real ear measurement headset

The tester headset for monitoring the patient
