Dedicated test box

TBS25 outperforms larger test boxes by 25-40 dB at low frequencies. The test box provides a perfect environment for hearing instrument testing (HIT) down to input levels of 35 dB SPL. Combine TBS25 with hearing aid fitting solutions from Interacoustics.

Built-in loop for telecoil testing

Valid testing of hearing instrument performance

Plugs for microphones, battery drain meters and external telecoil

ANSI and IEC compliable HIT measurements

Speaker in the same vertical plane as the hearing aid

TBS25 approximates free field conditions above 500 Hz and has its speaker in the same vertical plane as inserted hearing aids.

This facilitates testing of directional microphones.
