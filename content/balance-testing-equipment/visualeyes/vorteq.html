Taking VisualEyes™ to the next level

With the VORTEQ™ Assessments and Functional Assessments bundles, you gain access to the DVA, GST, and fvHIT™ functional VOR tests. The results from these tests provide an objective, data-driven starting point for vestibular therapy and the ability to monitor and adjust therapy to maximize progress.

To further your diagnostic capabilities, you can add the VORTEQ™ Diagnostics bundle to your VisualEyes™ 525 solution, allowing you to perform the Active Head Rotation (AHR) test and vHIT with your existing VNG goggle.
