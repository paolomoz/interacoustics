Move beyond VNG

VisualEyes™ offers VNG, vHIT, rotary chair testing, and functional VOR testing in one integrated system. With its comprehensive test battery, advanced technology, and seamless data management, VisualEyes™ enhances your diagnostic accuracy and efficiency.

VisualEyes™ 525 is our full-range VNG system, which offers balance centers, vestibular audiologists and neurologists the opportunity to quantify the functionality of the peripheral vestibular system and the central vestibular pathways. You can choose between various tracking algorithms that allow you to track all patients’ eyes to obtain more reliable test results.

Saccadometry, torsional eye tracking and optional functional VOR testing will help you to obtain an even more detailed understanding of your patients' vestibular disorders.
