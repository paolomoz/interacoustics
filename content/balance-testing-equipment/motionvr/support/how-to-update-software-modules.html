How to update software modules

Below, we will cover the following:

To perform these updates, you will need:

Access to an internet connection

A current software maintenance contract

If you do not have access to the internet or a current software maintenance contract, please contact your local sales representative to assist you.

When updates are available, you will see the following prompts:

An orange dot appears next to the settings tab on the left side of the screen.

A banner appears at the bottom of the software module you are using that requires an update.
