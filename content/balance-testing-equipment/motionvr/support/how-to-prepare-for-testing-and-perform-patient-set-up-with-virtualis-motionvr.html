Transcript

Before the patient arrives, set up the area and solution for use.

First, turn on the MotionVR system by turning the knob to the “ON” position.

The MotionVR force plate will move through a series of movements as part of the setup.

Make sure no one is standing on MotionVR during this calibration process.

Next, turn on the computer.

Turn on the VIVE goggle adapter.

Make sure the light turns green.

Wait until the SteamVR and VIVE applications open.
