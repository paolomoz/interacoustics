Steps to reconfigure VIVE Ultimate Trackers

This quick guide provides information on how to reconfigure the HTC Vive Ultimate Trackers.

If you need to reconfigure the trackers you will be automatically prompted. This usually occurs when you move the location of your Virtualis solution or change the room boundary. Note: if you move locations of your Virtualis solution, you will first be prompted to configure the room boundary. Complete the steps found in the “How to Reconfigure the Room Boundary” quick guide first.

If you move locations of your Virtualis solution but you are not prompted, first make sure the tracker dongle is inserted into the USB-c port on the HTC Vive Focus Vision VR headset and the trackers are turned on. If you are still not prompted, please refer to the troubleshooting quick guide.

1. Place the HTC Vive Focus Vision VR headset on your head.

2. You will then be prompted to reconfigure the trackers by scanning the room at low heights to ensure proper tracking below knee height. Using a controller, press “Start.”

3. Kneel to begin the tracker configuration.

4. Once in position, a pop-up will appear. Using a controller, press “Start scan.”

5. Look at the creature that appears in the tree trunks. Continue to focus on the creature until it disappears. You will need to complete this several times. Remain kneeling down through the entire scanning process.
