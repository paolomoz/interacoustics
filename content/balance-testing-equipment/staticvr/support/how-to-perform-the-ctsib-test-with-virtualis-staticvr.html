Transcript

This is how to perform the Clinical Test of Sensory Interaction on Balance (CTSIB) on the Virtualis StaticVR.

Today we will be doing the CTSIB to assess your balance.

The goal is I want you to stand as still as you can during the assessment.

Instruct the patient that their goal is to stand still during the assessment.

Have the patient remove their shoes for a standardized somatosensory input.

Center the patient's feet on the force plate.

The front edge of the medial malleolus of each foot should be directly centered on the horizontal line of the force plate.

When the feet are positioned correctly, assist the patient in putting on the headset.
