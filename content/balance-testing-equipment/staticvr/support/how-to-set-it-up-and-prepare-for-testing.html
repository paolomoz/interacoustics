Transcript

This is how to set up for using the StaticVR when performing an assessment.

Place the foam holder over the force plate to keep in a standardized position.

When performing a training module, you can position the force plates independently based on your therapy goals.

Once the area is set up, invite your patient over to the area.

Press ‘Tare’ before the patient steps onto the force plates for accurate data collection during the assessment.

Assist the patient onto the static force plates.

When performing assessment modules, position the front edge of the medial malleolus of each ankle aligned with a horizontal line on each force plate.

Next, place the headset on the patient and adjust to comfort.
