Equinox Evo & Touch Keyboard pediatric universe

Ease your workflow and ensure optimum patient care with the dedicated pediatric keyboard screens. You can use the Touch Keyboard wirelessly to stay close to and engage your patient while minimizing the risk of cueing.

Simplify your workflowEquinox Evo offers integrated VRA controls within the suite and Touch Keyboard. This eliminates the need for multiple systems and simplifies your workflow.

Ensure seamless coordination Equinox Evo provides a two-way communication system with wired headsets that work through the audiometer. Using this set-up, both audiologists can hear each other and the stimuli, ensuring seamless coordination.

Reduce your workload The automated ticksheet reduces your workload by automatically entering VRA responses into an integrated ticksheet. This provides you with a simple overview and record of the stimuli presentations and responses recorded.
