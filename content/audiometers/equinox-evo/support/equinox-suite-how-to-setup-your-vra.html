This is how you set up your VRA in the Equinox Suite.

When setting up the VRA for the Equinox Suite, go to the menu and select Setup | General Setup.

From here, choose the VRA system you are using, which can be either the analog VRA201 (teddy box) or the digital VRA Pure system that you can access through the suite.

Next, go to setup again and find the AC440 setup.

Here you can create a new protocol and rename it.

This will be a copy of your AC440 protocol with the same settings.

Then go to the Pediatric setup and find the add-ons for the VRA setting.

You can activate the number of rewards, indicating how many screens or teddy boxes you have connected to your system.
