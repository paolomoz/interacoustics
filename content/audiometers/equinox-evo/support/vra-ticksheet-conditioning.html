Before starting the pediatric module, go to Menu | Setup | AC440. Then create a protocol where VRA is activated. This is done in Protocols | Pediatric | Add-ons | VRA Settings.

When this is done and applied, the VRA controls will be available in the pediatric screen, together with the ticksheet and conditioning. Conditioning may also be activated from the pediatric screen on the Touch Keyboard.

To bring the ticksheet forward, click on the checkmark in the left side panel controls.

This will open the ticksheet in a new window.

When activating Conditioning by moving the slider to the right, the marks in the ticksheet will be set as a C. This allows the user to know when the child is being conditioned.

When conditioning is done and the child is ready to be tested, the conditioning slider is moved back and now there will be checkmarks and crosses for right and wrong reactions from the child.

The state of the measure (conditioning, checkmark or a cross) is stored on the ticksheet automatically.
