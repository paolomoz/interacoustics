Patient management software for audiologists

OtoAccess® Database is a software solution that covers the full line of Interacoustics' equipment that you may use in your practice. This includes the whole range of disciplines within audiology accompanied by vestibular solutions. OtoAccess® Database integrates your entire workflow in a single patient program. From here, you can create new patient records, perform your clinical tests, and access all your patient data.

With the ability to transfer data directly to Noah, OtoAccess® Database provides a smooth integration from electronic patient records to the ultimate hearing aid fitting. This feature facilitates quick and secure handling of data, thereby eliminating mistakes that may arise during the transfer of patient and test data.
